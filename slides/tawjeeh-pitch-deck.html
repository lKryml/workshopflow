From bad CVs + noisy job posts to trusted private-sector hiring.

Three-stage strategy: Seera AI (free CV builder) → AI jobboard + matching → full ATS + recruitment service.

Tawjeeh ATS

End-to-end hiring workflow for companies and agencies.

Problem

Libya hiring pain today

Unstructured
  • Job offers miss salary, role scope, and required skills.
  • Many listings exist only as social posts, hard to search/filter.
  • Candidates waste time applying to unclear opportunities.
Skills gap
  • Low awareness of ATS systems and CV screening logic.
  • Frequent CV quality issues: templates, language, formatting.
  • Employers struggle to identify fit quickly and fairly.

Context for pitch: unemployment remains high (working estimate: ~18–19%, verify latest official figure before live presentation). Government is actively pushing transition from public employment dominance toward private-sector growth.

Market insight

Why now

Public-sector dependency

User-provided context: ~80% of workers registered in public institutions, creating pressure on state payrolls.

Government momentum

Policy efforts focus on reducing unemployment and enabling private hiring channels.

Digital gap opportunity

Limited ATS knowledge and low-quality CVs create space for immediate, practical education + tools.

Tawjeeh positions itself as execution infrastructure for this transition: candidate readiness + employer quality + measurable matching.

Solution

3-stage product strategy

Stage 1: Seera AI

Free launch wedge

  • Convert bad CVs into ATS-ready format.
  • Generate new CVs + language correction.
  • Teach CV/ATS basics while collecting candidate profiles.

Stage 2: Jobboard AI

Core monetization

  • Build candidate persona from CV + cover letter + questionnaire.
  • Analyze job post + employer questionnaire + company vision.
  • Return transparent matching score to both sides.

Stage 3: ATS + Agency

Scale layer

  • Full ATS workflow for employers.
  • Recruitment service for talent agencies.
  • Data moat from prior stages improves hiring accuracy.
Business model

Who pays and why

Candidate side (free/low-friction)

  • Seera AI free for launch period to drive adoption.
  • Education-led trust: templates, ATS tips, language quality.
  • Large candidate DB built before heavy monetization.

Company side (credit model)

  • Employers buy credits to unlock applicant identities.
  • Before payment: profile shown with blurred personal data.
  • Visible pre-pay signals: match %, skills, persona highlights.

Outcome: candidate growth is protected, while company-side willingness to pay tracks hiring urgency and quality.
